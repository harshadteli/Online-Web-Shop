Our Story

Defining Time Since 1994

At SWAMI, we believe a watch is more than a tool for telling time—it is a legacy on your wrist. Founded on the principles of precision and timeless elegance, our workshop has dedicated decades to perfecting the art of horology.

Each timepiece is meticulously assembled by master watchmakers, ensuring that every gear, spring, and dial meets our rigorous standards of excellence.

Master Craftsmanship

Our artisans combine traditional Swiss techniques with modern engineering. From the hand-stitched leather straps to the scratch-resistant sapphire crystals, no detail is too small to be overlooked.


We source only the finest materials globally, ensuring that your SWAMI watch remains a functional piece of art for generations to come.
